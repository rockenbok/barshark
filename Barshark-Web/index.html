Our Vision.

We understand the difficult decisions involved when it comes to going out. Sometimes we find ourselves at a location that isn't quite what we were expecting. Wouldn’t it be great to know exactly what the outlook’s like before you get there? We’ll help by revealing drink and food specials, whether or not it’s busy, gender ratio, if your friends are checked in, an up/downvote system based on your experience and more! Let us be the go-to guide in order for you to have the best time.


-The Barshark Team

Real Time Updates

Real time updates show where’s busy and where’s not so busy. Using your phones' GPS data, Barshark automatically checks you into a location without releasing any of your personal data.

Gender Demographics

Barshark will automatically break down the age and gender averages of the location you’re scoping out. You’ll never show up with the wrong expectations again.

Rating System

You'll be able to see what others have rated a location as well as have the ability to submit your own experience with the simple touch of a finger.

Stay Connected

Your friends may already be out by the time you decide to go somewhere. If you want to meet up, see where they're are at through automatic check-ins without the need to call or text.

Slow or Busy

It’s hit or miss on some nights, so we’ll keep you informed on places that are both busy and not so busy.

Specials

Not sure about the food or drink specials at the spot you're planning on going to? Barshark will feature all local happy hours, specials and coupons at any sponsored locations.
